INTERNATIONAL

We want you to feel as comfortable as possible when you first move to America, so here is a list of resources on campus that can help you with your move! Check out these offices for more information!

OFFICE FOR INTERNATIONAL STUDENTS AND SCHOLARS

This office is the best place to go to for help with anything! The staff here are trained to help find courses that can help you learn English, travel around St. Louis, with documentation for work and international travel, and so much more. ENGLISH LANGUAGE PROGRAMS

The English Language Programs (ELP) provides classes and academic English language support designed to strengthen the English language proficiency of nonnative English speakers in the university community, and facilitate their academic and professional success at Washington University in St. Louis and beyond. PREPARE FOR THE USA

The best way to prepare for the US is to talk to current WashU students. There are many WashU student volunteers who would be more than happy to introduce students to the WashU lifestyle -- please contact the Undergraduate Admissions Office at (314) 935-6000 to ask for a student volunteer!

EXPLORE ST. LOUIS

We want students at WashU to explore St. Louis, especially because we are so lucky to be next to the amazing Loop and Forest Park. Check out just some of the attractions and restaurants St. Louis has to offer (other than the Arch)!
